About UniVerse

UniVerse was built by Nigerian university students who were tired of:

  • Outsiders in campus groups
  • Lecturers spying on WhatsApp
  • Dead Facebook pages and scattered chats
  • Apps full of ads and fake users

We made something better: a private social app only for verified university students.

Starting with UDUS and BUK — expanding to all Nigerian universities soon.

What makes UniVerse different

  • 100% campus-only — no outsiders ever
  • Anonymous posting available
  • Channels for everything: memes, confessions, academics
  • Stories that disappear in 24 hours
  • No ads. No data selling. No nonsense.
