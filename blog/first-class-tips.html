How I Graduated with First Class Real Tips from a 4.72 CGPA Holder

By BTK • December 24, 2025

I graduated with 4.72 CGPA from UDUS (Class of '25). No rich parents, no genius brain just strategy and consistency.

Here are the real tips that worked for me (not the "read 12 hours a day" lies).

1. Understand the System First

Know how CA and exam marks are distributed. Some courses, CA is 40% focus there. Past questions are gold.

2. Study Smart, Not Hard

Read to understand, not to cram. Join serious group discussions teach others to remember better.

3. Build Relationship with Lecturers

Ask questions in class. Visit office hours. They remember you during marking (bonus marks dey).

4. Time Management & Health

Sleep well. Eat properly. Exercise. Burnout will kill your CGPA faster than hard courses.

5. Consistency Over Intensity

Study small every day instead of all night before exam. Your brain retains better.

First Class is possible for anyone willing to work smart. You don't need to be genius just disciplined.
