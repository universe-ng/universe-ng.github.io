The Eternal Struggle: Nigerian Student Hunger Hits Different

Hunger in Nigerian university no be joke. E dey hit different levels. üò©üçö

From the moment your pocket money finishes till the next alert, hunger becomes your closest companion. Let's laugh (and cry) through the stages every student knows too well.

1. The Cafeteria Rice Betrayal

You order "rice and beans" with hope in your heart. What you get? Mount Everest of rice with exactly 5 beans scattered like confetti. You count them one by one like treasure. "Na decoration?"

2. Garri: The Ultimate Survivor Food

Garri is not food it's a lifestyle. Soak am with cold water and sugar. Fry am when light come. Drink am like tea when money no dey. Garri has saved more students than scholarship.

3. Indomie Upgrades & Concoction Magic

When small change land, you upgrade Indomie with egg, sardine, or even plantain. When pocket dry? Concoction rice: rice + maggi + pepper + anything wey remain for pot.

4. The "Abeg Borrow Me 200" Era

You turn professional beggar: "Guy abeg borrow me 200 make I hold body." Bread and pure water become luxury. You calculate how many days left till end of month like mathematician.

5. Holiday Dreams & Mummy's Jollof Countdown

Every night you dream of real food. You count days till you go home: "Only 12 days till mummy's jollof and chicken." That's the only motivation to survive finals.
