Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 December 2025

UniVerse is designed with student privacy in mind. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we work to protect it.

Information we collect

  • Account Information: Basic details (such as email address, pasword, username and full name no phone number) if you choose to create an account.
  • User Content: Content you choose to share on the platform, including posts, messages, and profile details.

How information is used

  • To provide and maintain core app functionality.
  • To support campus based interactions and moderation.
  • To improve reliability, performance, and user experience.

Data storage and security

  • User data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure (including Supabase).
  • Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access.

Third party services

UniVerse may rely on trusted third party services for infrastructure and app functionality. These services are used only as necessary to operate the platform.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. Information is shared only when required to operate the service or comply with applicable laws.

Your choices

You may choose what information to share on UniVerse and can request account deletion by contacting us.
